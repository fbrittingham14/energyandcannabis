Your Cannabis Isn’t As Green As You Think

Why sun-grown weed is better for the planet and your conscience

Where Are These Emissions Coming From?

Indoor cannabis cultivation has become the dominant method since legalization, driving industry emissions through the roof.

How Bad Is It, Really?

Producing one 1-gram joint indoors releases the same emissions as driving a car 12 miles, compared to just 2 miles for outdoor-grown cannabis.

Why Are They Growing Indoors at All?

Indoor grows offer consistency, higher THC levels, and more harvest cycles per year, but they come with major environmental costs.
